Biography

My name is Zixing Lei(雷梓行) and you can just call me Elwood. Currently I am a master student in the Cooperative Medianet Innovation Center(CMIC) of Shanghai Jiao Tong University(SJTU), supervised by Prof. Siheng Chen. My research interests include computer vision, embodied AI and multi-robots perception.
